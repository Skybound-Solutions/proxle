Welcome to **Proxle** ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our word-guessing game at [https://proxle.app](https://proxle.app) (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information from Google Account (OAuth)

When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used to identify your account
- **Display name**: Shown in the app (if we implement leaderboards in the future)
- **Profile photo**: May be displayed in your user profile
- **Google User ID**: A unique identifier to link your game progress

**We only request these basic scopes. We DO NOT access:**
- ‚ùå Your Gmail or email contents
- ‚ùå Your Google Drive files
- ‚ùå Your YouTube data
- ‚ùå Your Calendar or Contacts
- ‚ùå Any other Google services beyond basic profile information

### 2. Game Data

We automatically collect and store:
- **Game statistics**: Number of games played, wins, losses, current streak, maximum streak
- **Guess history**: Your guesses and results for statistical purposes (anonymized)
- **Daily completion status**: Whether you've completed today's puzzle (to enforce one-game-per-day rule)
- **Timestamps**: When you played each game

### 3. Device Information

We may collect:
- **Browser type** and version
- **Device type** (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **IP address** (automatically logged by Firebase/Google Cloud)

### 4. Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand:
- How many people play the game
- Which features are used most
- Where errors occur
- App performance metrics

**This data is anonymized and aggregated.**

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored securely using:
- **Firebase Authentication** (Google Cloud Platform)
- **Cloud Firestore** (Google Cloud Platform)
- **Servers located in**: United States (`us-central1` region)

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data is encrypted at rest on Google Cloud servers
- Authentication tokens use OAuth 2.0 standard
- Access to databases is restricted and monitored

However, **no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Game statistics**: Indefinitely, unless you request deletion
- **Authentication tokens**: Managed automatically by Firebase (expire/refresh as needed)
- **Logs**: Up to 30 days for debugging purposes
